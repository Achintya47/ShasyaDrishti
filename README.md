## 🔍 Predictive Pipelines

### 1. Commodity Price Prediction
Forecasts crop prices (e.g., brinjal, potato) to align production with market demand and improve profitability.

### 2. Rainfall Prediction
Models temporal climate variability to assist in irrigation planning and risk mitigation.

### 3. AQI Prediction
Captures environmental stress factors affecting crop health and productivity.

### 4. Crop Yield Prediction
Integrates AQI and rainfall signals to estimate yield, treating agriculture as a system-level outcome.

---

## 🧠 Key Insight
Agriculture is not static—it is influenced by **climate uncertainty, environmental conditions, and market fluctuations**.  
ShasyaDrishti shifts from isolated predictions to a **holistic, system-driven approach**.
